ABOUT THE PROGRAM

AET is a school located in Northern VA as part of the Academies of Loudoun. The information Technology pathway is one of the three pathways (enineering and entrepreneurship being the other two) that sutdents can choose to be a part of. The IT pathway itself, specifically focuses on building skills in programming, web design, and other aspects of technology to allow students to gain a broad insight of the field. With many research opportunities, students are able to partake in their own experimental thinking that corresponds with the design process.
